Director's Message

Indian Companies. Indian professionals have also risen to the challenges of the sector and have stimulated and spearheaded its development to put India on the pharmaceutical map of the world.

The pharmaceutical industry in India is developing at a galloping pace to catch up with the global markets and to register a significant presence in the globalised scenario. It is a matter of great national pride that many of our companies are enjoying the status of preferred partner by global giants. In this scenario, India’s academic institutes have a crucial role to play in equipping our students adequately to live up to this grand vision and to enable the industry to meet these challenges.

Truba Institute of Pharmacy offers a 4 year degree academic course, 2 years post graduate courses in Pharmaceutics and Pharmacology and 2 years Diploma course in Pharmacy. The College is provided with the latest infrastructure and equipment’s and we have put in place highly qualified dedicated teachers so that the best can be offered to our students. Along with the academic pursuits, the Institute, within the umbrella carries out various extra curricular and cultural activities aimed at total personality development of the students. Our aim is to help our students to develop an intelligent mind with analytical ability so that they always remain in the forefront in whatever pursuit they undertake. In the next few years, the demand for pharmacists and pharmaceutical scientists is expected to grow from 500,000 to almost 1.5 million. In short, our aim is to create a top class College of Pharmacy that will train future pharmacists who will be committed to the best possible pharmaceutical care to the community and to meet the demands of pharmaceutical industry.

Dr. Surendra K. Jain
