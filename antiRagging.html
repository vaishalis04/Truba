Say NO to Ragging!

Ragging is a damaging form of interaction in educational institutions that subjects new students to physical and psychological abuse. Originally intended to build camaraderie, it has devolved into harmful practices. The Supreme Court of India mandates strict measures against ragging, including the National Anti-Ragging Helpline.
