The Truba Institute of Pharmacy, Bhopal, has achieved accreditation from the National Board of Accreditation (NBA) for its Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharm) program, underscoring its commitment to excellence in pharmaceutical education. This prestigious accreditation signifies that the institute meets high standards of quality in education, infrastructure, faculty, and curriculum, aligning with global benchmarks. NBA accreditation enhances the credibility of the program, ensuring students receive a robust academic foundation and practical training, preparing them for successful careers in the pharmaceutical industry. It also reflects the institute's dedication to continuous improvement and delivering value-based education.
