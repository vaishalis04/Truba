Institution’s Innovation Council (IIC)

Ministry Of Education Institution’s Innovation Council (MoE-IIC)

Introduction

The Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD), Government of India, established the MHRD’s Innovation Cell (MIC) to systematically foster a culture of innovation in Higher Education Institutions (HEIs). MIC aims to encourage and nurture young students by supporting them in developing new ideas during their formative years through Institution’s Innovation Councils (IICs).

  • Smart India Hackathon (SIH) 2020
  • Institution Innovation Councils (IIC)
  • Atal Ranking of Institutions on Innovation Achievements (ARIIA)
  • National Innovation and Start-up Policy for Students and Faculties

Objectives

  • Conduct innovation and entrepreneurship activities prescribed by Central MIC.
  • Identify and reward innovations, share success stories.
  • Organize workshops, seminars, and interactions with entrepreneurs and investors.
  • Create a mentor pool for student innovators.
  • Network with national entrepreneurship organizations.
  • Organize hackathons, competitions, and industry-involved challenges.

Benefits

  • Utilizes existing local ecosystem without major capital investment.
  • Exclusive participation in MoE innovation initiatives.
  • Exciting prizes and certificates every month.
  • Interact with business leaders and academicians.
  • Mentoring by industry professionals.
  • Experiment with new technologies.
  • Travel and cultural exchange opportunities.
